<h1>Head of School (HoS) Portal</h1>
<P>Welcome to the new HoS portal. 
This portal has been created for your convenience.
 The data presented are the most up to date datasets as they appear in the MIU system. We are aware that
 some of the data is not entirely correct and we have designed this portal in such a way that you
 can not only look at all the relevant information for your school, but that you can also help us
 check all data sets on their correctness, so that, together, we can continue to improve the in
formation systems at Wits.</P>
