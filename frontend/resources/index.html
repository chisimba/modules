The Payment PebbleTM API

Accept card payments in a mobile app

Are you a developer who wants to accept card payments with a mobile app? Do you want to make this as easy as possible, something to be done with an hour or two of work? This is now possible with the PaymentPebbleTM Simple API!

The thumbzup PaymentPebbleTM Simple API allows rapid addition of card payments to apps for Android, iOS, and Blackberry 10 in a secure and industry standards-compliant manner.

How it works

Your app sends a request to the PaymentPebbleTM app requesting it to handle a payment transaction.

The PaymentPebbleTM app carries out the transaction, and returns the result to your app.

You can verify the transaction against our application server using a simple RESTful call.

Your app presents the result to the customer and merchant.
